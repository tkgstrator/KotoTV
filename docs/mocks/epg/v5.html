v5 Design note:
Mobile-native single-channel view. The channel hopper (pill strip) lets users jump between channels without leaving the page. Each channel shows: ON AIR hero with progress bar + watch CTA, then future programs listed vertically with time, then past programs (dimmed).

On desktop ≥768px, the mobile frame appears as a phone-shaped card on the left. A future iteration could show this layout in a sidebar while the right panel shows a classic horizontal grid — bridging mobile and desktop UX.

Trade-off: No cross-channel comparison possible (can't scan "what's on at 22:00 across all channels"). Best for users who have a preferred channel in mind.
